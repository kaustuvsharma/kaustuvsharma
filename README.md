## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🎯 VerifiedTutor: a faithfulness-verified academic tutor
A pedagogy-aware Q&A tutor for Class 12 Computer Science that runs entirely on a 6 GB consumer GPU.
- Hybrid **RAG** (MiniLM + TF-IDF, k=5) over a lecture corpus, generation with **Qwen 2.5 1.5B**.
- Verifies **every generated claim** against its sources with a **DeBERTa-v3 NLI** cross-encoder, surfaces a calibrated **HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW** confidence band, and refuses off-topic or prompt-injection inputs before they ever reach the model.
- Deployed on **Google Cloud Run**, scales to zero for **$0 idle cost**.
- Closes five cited research gaps in educational AI: faithfulness, calibration, scaffolding, privacy, and lost-in-the-middle.
- **Stack:** `Python` · `PyTorch` · `Transformers` · `Flask + SSE` · `Docker` · `Google Cloud`
- 🔗 [verifiedtutor-capstone](https://github.com/kaustuvsharma/verifiedtutor-capstone)
